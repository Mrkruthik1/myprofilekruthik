production is a multi-stage process that can take years to complete. The five stages of film production are:

Development: The first stage of film production

Pre-production: The team finalizes aspects related to shooting the film

Production: The film is shot on stage, in booths, or at locations

Post-production: Color correction is carried out before visual effects but after editing

Distribution: The film is distributed or exhibited

Each stage has a different purpose, and different roles suit different stages. The stages vary in length, and sometimes a film will never even find its way out of the first phase.

Movie production involves:

Scriptwriting

Planning

Shooting

Financial funding

Hiring the film crew

Finding the locations

Marketing

Other aspects of movie production include:

Director of photography (DOP)

Production sound mixer

Sound recordist

StudioBinder
